Frequently Asked Questions

How can I earn volunteer hours on FreeTutors?

To earn volunteer hours on FreeTutors you have to sign up as a tutor. Then, for every five questions you answer, one volunteer hour will be added to your roster.

What subjects or topics does Freetutors cover?

The topics covered by FreeTutors include: Math, Chemistry, Biology, Physics, English, History, Geography, Foreign Language, Computer Science, and Physical Education. If you want to request a new subject, visit our contact us page.

How do I type special characters on FreeTutors?

FreeTutors uses MathJax to provide Latex integration on our site. To write inline math, type your Latex code between two dollar signs. Ex. $\sum_1^10 x^2$.

Can I attach files or images to my questions/answers on FreeTutors?

To attach an image to your question/answer on FreeTutors click the image button on the editor or simply drag your image into the editor. Files are currently not supported on FreeTutors.

Can I edit or delete my questions or answers on Freetutors?

Editing/deleting your questions/answers is currently not supported on FreeTutors.

How can I provide feedback or report issues with the Freetutors website?

To provide feedback or report issues, visit the suggestions page. To contact us for any other reason visit the contact us page.
