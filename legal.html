Data We Collect From You

  1. Full Name: We collect your full name as entered on the sign-up page to personalize your user experience and address you appropriately.
  2. Email Address: We collect your email address as entered on the sign-up page to facilitate communication, send important updates, and verify your account.
  3. Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and provide personalized features. These cookies allow us to log you back in automatically when you return to our website. Please note that upon visiting our website, a banner will appear after five seconds to inform you that cookies are collected. Cookies are required for the site to function properly and are mandatory.

In order to keep your information secure, we use AWS Cognito to encrypt it, making it more secure.


Please review our "Cookies and Tracking Technologies" section for more details on how we use cookies and your options for managing them.

Why We Collect Your Data

We collect your data for the following purposes:

  1. User Account Management: The information we collect, including your full name, email address, and username, is used for the management of your user account on our website. This allows us to provide you with access to personalized features, maintain your account security, and deliver a seamless user experience.
  2. Communication: We may use your email address to communicate with you regarding important updates, account-related notifications, and inquiries.
  3. Personalization: By collecting certain data, such as your full name, we can personalize your user experience and address you appropriately when using our website.
  4. Login Persistence: The use of cookies enables us to remember your login session, allowing you to access your account quickly and efficiently when returning to our website.

Please note that we do not share your data with third parties except as described in the "Data Sharing and Disclosure" section and the "Third-Party Services" section of this privacy policy.

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and provide personalized features. By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this section. Cookies are used for the following purposes:

  1. Authentication and Session Management: We use cookies to keep you logged in and maintain your session across different pages of our website. This allows you to access your account and personalized features without having to log in repeatedly.
  2. Analytical Purposes: We may utilize cookies to gather information about how you interact with our website, such as the pages you visit, the links you click, and other actions you take. This information helps us analyze and improve the performance and functionality of our website.

Please review the privacy policies of these third parties to understand their data collection and usage practices. A banner will appear upon visiting our website, notifying you that cookies are collected. Please note that you will not have the option to decline the use of cookies.

Third-Party Services

We utilize third-party services to enhance the functionality and performance of our website. These services may collect and process certain personal information on our behalf. By using our website, you acknowledge and agree that your data may be shared with the following third-party services:

  1. Amazon Web Services (AWS): We rely on AWS for hosting our website and utilizing several of their services to ensure a secure and efficient user experience. The AWS services we use include:
    1. AWS Amplify: We leverage AWS Amplify to streamline the development and deployment of our web application.
    2. AWS Cognito: We utilize AWS Cognito for user authentication and authorization. This service securely manages user account information, including the hashing of passwords, which ensures that nobody has access to your actual password.
    3. AWS DynamoDB: We utilize AWS DynamoDB to store certain user profile information such as the profile picture, email, full name, questions, answers, about me section, and username. DynamoDB helps us efficiently store and retrieve this information while ensuring scalability and data integrity.
    4. AWS Lambda: We use AWS Lambda to process users' questions and answers, and to compute various miscellaneous tasks. This service allows us to execute code in a scalable and efficient manner, enabling the processing and analysis of user-generated content.
    For more information on how AWS collects and uses your data, refer to the AWS Privacy Notice.
  2. Google AdSense: We have implemented Google AdSense on our website to display advertisements. Google AdSense may collect certain information about your interactions with the ads, such as your IP address and browsing behavior, to provide personalized advertisements. Please refer to Google AdSense's privacy policy for more information on how they collect, use, and protect your data.

Please note that while we have reputable third-party service providers, we do not have direct control over their data practices. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these third-party services to understand how they handle and protect your personal information.

Keeping Your Data Secure

We take the security of your personal data seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect it. We have implemented several precautions designed to prevent unauthorized access, use, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of your personal information.

Here are some of the measures we have in place to keep your data secure:

  1. Encryption: We utilize AWS Cognito to manage and store passwords, which uses encryption techniques such as secure socket layer (SSL) technology, to protect the transmission of data between your device and our website. Additionally, all passwords are hashed before storing, which means nobody has access to the actual password.
  2. Data Minimization: We only collect and retain the personal data that is necessary for the purposes outlined in this privacy policy. No additional data is collected for marketing purposes.

While we strive to implement and maintain robust security measures, no method of transmission or storage can guarantee absolute security. However, we continuously monitor and update our security practices to protect your personal information to the best of our abilities.
